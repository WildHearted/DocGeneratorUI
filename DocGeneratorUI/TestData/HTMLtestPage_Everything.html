Dimension Data will monitor and manage the availability of configuration items (device) and the network, as well as capacity utilisation and technology service performance, by setting thresholds for configuration items and services as defined by the client.
Availability, capacity and performance-related events (whether informational or exceptions such as failures) are recorded and managed and, if required (in the case of exceptions), logged and addressed as an incident (see Incident Management).
It is critical however, for any event that affect networks, that the client be notified within 15 minutes of the actual detection of the ’failure‘ by our systems – referred as proactive notification. This notification can be via email or SMS.
Dimension Data will assist the client in determining the best threshold for each configuration item, instrumenting and monitoring services from network edge to network edge (Dimension Data’s scope of responsibility).
